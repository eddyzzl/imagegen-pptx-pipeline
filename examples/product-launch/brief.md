# Product Launch Deck Example

Use `$imagegen-pptx-pipeline` to create a 10-slide product launch deck.

## Brief

We are launching a workflow automation product for operations teams. The audience is an executive product review committee. The deck should explain the market problem, product positioning, differentiated workflow, proof of value, launch plan, risk controls, and decision request.

## Constraints

- Use a premium but practical executive style.
- Generate 4 materially different ImageGen visual directions.
- Confirm `slide_intent_matrix.md` before narrative options.
- Confirm `narrative_matrix.md` before style exploration.
- Final PPTX must keep titles, key numbers, footers, and main text editable.

## Suggested Source Placeholders

- Product screenshots
- Launch timeline
- Beta user metrics
- Pricing or packaging notes
- Brand assets
